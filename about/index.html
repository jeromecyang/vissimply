visualize simply

About

Visualize data simply means to make them easy-to-build, succinct and comprehensible. A blog authored by Jerome Yang, a UI/UX, visualization, map and graphic enthusiast.

Educated in geographic information science (GIS) and cartography, Jerome is currently a full-stack software developer focusing on interactive web interface and visualization. Previously he was a product engineer at Esri and developed ArcGIS JavaScript API as well as ArcGIS online. His goal is to research and develop tools that empower people (both technical and non-technical) to speak through visualized data.
